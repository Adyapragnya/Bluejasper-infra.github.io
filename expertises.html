Our Expertise


We view assets as anything of value to you whose maintenance involves getting the most out of them to produce optimal outcomes for your customers and stakeholders. All assets require some type of work performed on them to keep them operable. Such work typically involves tracking location, labor, material, equipment, inspections and condition assessments to maximize your maintenance and lifecycle goals. The approach taken in advancing these goals may be informed by best practices, standard operating procedures, regulatory compliance, budgetary considerations and resource levels.

However, every organization carries with it a unique set of capabilities and challenges that make up the institutional knowledge critical to driving the desired outcomes. The BlueJasper team recognizes and is guided foremost by this institutional wisdom to help deliver solutions that are aligned with organizational needs and priorities.

Complex Problems, Realistic Solutions Our Path to Clarity...


We understand that the need for higher capability and specialization adds a certain level of complexity to the growth of any solution. However, we also believe that all complex things are best handled by breaking them into smaller, manageable tasks they are made up of in the first place. This allows us to take a realistic approach to solving problems for achieving realistic outcomes. It is this focus on keeping things simple that makes BlueJasper a practical solution, providing clarity to your operations.

Whole of Operations Approach - The BlueJasper platform is being designed with flexibility and extensibility in mind. Anchored by assets and their locational (GIS) environment as its foundational components, the platform would also include support for other related aspects of an organization's operations including citizen (civic) engagement, land management, permitting and business licensing. Further, it would have the ability to interact with enterprise systems such as utility billing, ERP as well as external applications. All of this means, we are with you in adopting a 'whole of operations' approach to help manage your infrastructure and community development needs with a strategic perspective.
